❌ Commercial Companies - NO ACCESS
❌ Institutions         - NO ACCESS
❌ Governments          - NO ACCESS

WHY THIS CHANGE:
Verum Omnis is designed to PROTECT INDIVIDUALS, not empower institutions.

The tool must serve ordinary people who need it to:
- Protect themselves
- Prove truth
- Assert rights
- Resist abuse
- Maintain freedom

In the hands of corporations/governments, it could:
- Suppress dissent
- Control populations
- Concentrate power
- Exploit vulnerable people

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY:
Power should be in the hands of individuals, not institutions.
